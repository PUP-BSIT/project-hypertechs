Cookie Use

Apex Bank's uses cookies to ensure you have the best browsing experience on our banking system. By using our site, you acknowledge that you have read and understood our Cookie Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device that help us improve your experience on our Platform.

2. Types of Cookies

We use both session and persistent cookies. Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a longer period.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us analyze usage patterns, provide personalized content, and improve our services. We do not use cookies for any purpose other than those stated in this policy.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services integrated into our Platform may also use cookies. We have no control over these cookies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on the Platform..

6. Updates to Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes, and continued use of the Platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the modified policy.
